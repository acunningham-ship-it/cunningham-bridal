Her story

It started with a cover girl.

As a young girl, Kelly would spend hours experimenting with her hair and makeup, turning herself into the latest cover girl. It brought her joy — and she wanted others to feel that same joy on their most important days.

Now thirteen years into the industry, she's the go-to for brides across New Jersey for all things hair and makeup — trusted for looks that photograph beautifully and hold from the first look to the last dance.
